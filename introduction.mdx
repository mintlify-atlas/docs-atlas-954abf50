# Private transaction submission for Reth

Extend [Reth](https://github.com/paradigmxyz/reth) with a custom RPC method that allows you to bypass the public mempool and send transactions directly to block builders, protecting against frontrunning and sandwich attacks.

## What is reth-private-transaction?

This project adds `eth_sendPrivateRawTransaction` to the standard Ethereum JSON-RPC API, enabling private transaction submission directly to trusted block builders. By keeping your transactions out of the public mempool until they're included in a block, you significantly reduce the risk of MEV extraction.

<Note>
When you send a transaction to the public mempool, it becomes visible to searchers and MEV bots who can frontrun, backrun, or sandwich your transaction. Private transaction submission prevents this by sending your transaction directly to block builders.
</Note>

The extension broadcasts your signed transaction to multiple block builders simultaneously:

## Why privacy matters

The public Ethereum mempool is transparent by design. While this transparency is valuable for network health, it creates opportunities for MEV extraction:

- **Frontrunning**: Bots see your transaction and submit their own with higher gas to execute first
- **Sandwich attacks**: Your swap gets sandwiched between a frontrun buy and backrun sell, extracting value
- **Transaction sniping**: High-value opportunities get stolen before your transaction executes

Private transaction submission solves these problems by keeping your transaction invisible until it's safely included in a block.
